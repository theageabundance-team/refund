01What is Green Tracker?∧
Green Tracker is your membership to the fund recovery system. Once you're inside, you get access to the Fund Verification tool that searches federal and state unclaimed fund databases using your name, plus a full library of training videos and bonus courses covering financial recovery, PayPal earnings, Amazon affiliate marketing, and digital marketing. Everything in one place, built to put money back in your pocket.
02How do I access my funds?∨
Log into your dashboard and start with the Fund Verification tool. Enter your name and let the system search federal and state databases for unclaimed amounts tied to your identity. From there, watch the training videos inside your dashboard. Completing them advances your processing date and moves your funds closer to release. Most members who follow the steps consistently see their withdrawal cleared within the standard 60-day window.
03How long until my funds are available?∨
The standard processing window is 60 days from your verification date. Every training video and course you complete inside the dashboard advances your processing date. Members who go through the full training consistently move through the window faster than those who don't.
04Why do I need to complete the training videos?∨
The training is what advances your processing date inside the system. Think of it as the verification layer that confirms your request is active and moving through the pipeline. Members who complete the training consistently reach their withdrawal date faster. It's also genuinely useful, covering how to maximize your fund recovery and build additional income streams.

Yes, completely. ClickBank and Explodely are the payment processors that handle your Green Tracker membership. When you see either of those charges, it confirms your membership is active and your fund recovery request is in the system. If you have any questions, email systemushelp@gmail.com.
06Is my personal information safe?∨
Yes. All data is protected with 256-bit SSL encryption, the same standard used by major financial institutions. Your information is never sold or shared with third parties. We only use your name to search the unclaimed fund databases on your behalf.
